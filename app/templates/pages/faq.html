Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is OpenKin?

OpenKin is a tool that helps you create, organize, and visualize your family's genealogical history.

How do I start creating my family tree?

Start by gathering information about your family members, such as names, birthdates, and relationships. Then, input this information into OpenKin to begin building your tree.

Can I add photos to my family tree?

Yes, OpenKins allows you to add photos of your family members to enhance your tree and make it more visually appealing.

Is my family tree data private?

OpenKin offers privacy controls to help you manage who can view and access your tree.

Can I share my family tree with others?

Yes, OpenKin provides options to share your tree with family members and friends via email or social media.

What if I make a mistake on my family tree?

You can edit or delete information in your family tree maker to correct any mistakes or update information.

Using OpenKin

How do I sign up for OpenKin?

To sign up for OpenKin, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and follow the instructions to create an account.

How much does it cost to use OpenKin?

OpenKin offers a free basic plan as well as premium plans with additional features. Visit our pricing page for more details.

Can I import data from other family tree software?

Yes, OpenKin allows you to import GEDCOM files, which are standard genealogical data files used by many family tree software programs.

How do I contact customer support?

You can contact OpenKin customer support via email at support@openkin.com or through the contact form on our website.
