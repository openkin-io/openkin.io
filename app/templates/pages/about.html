About OpenKin

Connecting Families Across Generations

Our Mission

At OpenKin, our mission is to help you discover, create, and share your family's unique story. We believe in the power of connections and the importance of preserving family heritage for future generations.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We are committed to honesty and transparency in all our endeavors.
  • Innovation: We continuously strive to improve and innovate our services.
  • Community: We value the sense of belonging and community among our users.
  • Excellence: We aim to provide the highest quality tools and resources for family tree building.
